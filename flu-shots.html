Flu Shot

Did you know that no matter where you work or what you do at Sherwin-Williams you and your dependent family members are eligible for a FREE flu shot? The flu shot program runs annually, each fall. During the flu shot program (September 1 – December 31), here is how you can get your flu shot:

  • On-site: Flu  shot  clinics  are  scheduled in the fall at certain facilities with 100 or more employees.
  • Flu Shot Vouchers: Vouchers are available on www.myswbenefits.com. Print your flu shot voucher(s) for yourself and eligible family members that will be accepted at any CVS or Walgreens nationwide. One voucher per eligible dependent.
  • This last option is available year-round and during the annual flu shot program:
  • Visit your health care provider: Employees and dependents enrolled in a Sherwin-Williams medical plan
  • can visit a primary care doctor and obtain a flu shot at no cost.

Employees do not have to be enrolled in a Sherwin-Williams health care plan to participate. Eligible dependents include spouse, domestic partner, dependent children and children of domestic partners. The minimum age requirement for children varies by state.
