Weight Watchers reimagined

Want to live a longer, healthier life? Start with the number

on the scale. We  want  to  help  you  get on track to a healthy weight and a healthier lifestyle. Achieving a healthy weight not only reduces your risk of heart disease, diabetes and certain types of cancer, it also helps you gain energy, self-confidence and improves your quality of life. The right time to be healthier is now—and we want to help.

Weight Watchers is a scientifically-proven approach to weight loss based on eating healthy, being active and learning how to make the small changes that can lead to lasting weight loss.

The Sherwin-Williams Weight Watchers program, which is available to regular full- and regular part-time, temp, co-op and intern employees, spouses, domestic partners, dependent children and domestic partner children (over age 18 through the end of the month they turn 25), includes three options:

  • Monthly Pass
  • Online subscription; or
  • At Work meetings

And, to help you make the commitment to healthy weight loss, Sherwin-Williams will reimburse 80% of the cost of your Weight Watchers option upon successful program completion. The annual maximum reimbursement is $200 per year.

To enroll in this program, log on to www.myswbenefits.com and register on the new Weight Watchers online portal. You can call the Weight Watchers hotline at 866-204-2885 with any questions on the program or portal.
