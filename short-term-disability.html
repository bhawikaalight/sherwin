Short Term Disability

You are automatically enrolled in the Short-Term Disability plan as a regular full-time employee after completion of one year of full-time service. If you go from part-time to full-time status, you become eligible for this benefit after you complete one year of continuous full-time service. The plan is fully paid by Sherwin-Williams.
