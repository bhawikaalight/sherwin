When Should I Enroll in Health and Welfare Benefits?

You have 30 days from the first day of your full-time status or ACA Eligible Date to complete your benefits enrollment or your electable benefits will be waived and you will not have coverage. The next opportunity to enroll in health care benefits is the annual Open Enrollment held each October/November for the following plan year or when you experience a qualified status change.
