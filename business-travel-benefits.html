Business Travel Benefits

Business Travel Accident Insurance is available to full-time, salaried employees at no cost to employees. This benefit provides coverage for loss of life payable at three (3) times your salary with a minimum payment of $100,000 and a maximum payment of $1,000,000. “Business Travel” is defined as a trip authorized by the Company for the purpose of furthering the business of the Company while away from your regular place of work. The Business Travel Accident Plan also covers accidental death and dismemberment that occurred during the business travel. This benefit is available for employees only (does not cover dependents).

World Business Traveler

Plan The Sherwin-Williams World Traveler plan provides emergency medical and dental coverage while traveling outside the employee's home country on an authorized company busniess trip — including doctor visits, prescription drug coverage, and inpatient hospital expenses for urgent and emergency care.

“Business Travel” for the World Business Traveler Plan is defined as Business travel outside your home country for a maximum of 180 consecutive days for any one Business Trip, with no more than 270 travel days in a 12 month period. If your country of residence and/or domicile is the United States or any U.S. Territory/Protectorate, travel between any combination of the 50 United States and U.S. Territories/Protectorates is considered travel within the home country.

  • The plan is offered through Aetna International
  • There is no cost to the employee for this plan

The following are eligible for coverage:

  • Employee: Regular full-time employee under age 70
  • Dependent: Spouse or domestic partner under age 70, unmarried children to the covered employee. Dependents are covered as long as they are traveling with the covered employee on an authorized company business trip

International SOS

Sherwin-Williams partners with International SOS, the leading medical assistance, international health care and security assistance company. Travel security information is provided by a joint venture of International SOS and Control Risks, the world’s leading security risk management firm.

Rest assured that whenever you travel internationally on company business, Sherwin-Williams has a 24/7 resource on call, online and even on the ground to help with any medical, security and logistical questions, concerns and situations that may arise. So if you need a medical referral, lose your medication, seek pre- travel advice or experience a medical or security crisis, Sherwin-Williams has a fully integrated program in place so you receive the care and expertise that you need, whenever you need it.

* Collectively Bargained Employees are NOT eligible for Business Travel Accident Insurance.
