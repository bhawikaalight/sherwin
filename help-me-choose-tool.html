Help Me Choose

Use this online tool—available on www.myswbenefits.com—to help you choose your benefits. This resource is:

  • Easy to use. A virtual advisor leads you through an interactive session explaining your options simply and quickly.
  • Focused on you. They ask questions and you answer. Your answers help to identify recommendations based on your needs.
  • Like having your own personal advisor. They help you sort through a lot of information in stages that make sense, at your pace.
  • Private. The answers you enter are used only to help you choose a plan. They are not stored or used for anything else.
  • Fun. Help Me Choose makes choosing a health care plan kind of fun. Really.
