Get Help. Stay Quit.

We know quitting tobacco isn’t easy, and we’re here to help. The Quit for Life Program has helped thousands of people take control of their lives and health by quitting tobacco.

The telephone-based and online coaching treatment program is available free of charge for all regular full- and regular part-time, temp, co-op and intern U.S. and Canadian employees, spouses, domestic partners, dependent children and domestic partner children (over age 18 through the end of the month they turn 25).
It’s free, it’s confidential, and it works.

Upon successful completion of this program, you qualify for the Non-Tobacco discount if you and all of your covered dependents have been tobacco free for the prior six months.

You can enroll in this program by calling 866-QUIT-4-LIFE (866-784-8454) or log on to www.quitnow.net/Sherwin and follow the instructions.
